About Us

Country in a Box was born from a simple idea: travel the world through food without leaving your home!

We bring authentic flavors from around the world straight to your table.

Our Mission

To deliver authentic international cuisines with uncompromising quality, bringing the world's flavors to every Filipino home. We are committed to excellence in taste, service, and customer satisfaction, making global gastronomy accessible and affordable for everyone.

Our Vision

To become the leading restaurant in the Philippines, recognized for our diverse menu offerings and exceptional quality. We envision a future where every household can experience the richness of world cuisines, fostering cultural appreciation through food and creating memorable dining experiences.
